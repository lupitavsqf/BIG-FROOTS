About Us

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to conscious design—using technology ethically to support mental health and neurodivergent inclusion. Many modern devices are designed to maximize engagement, often causing cognitive strain, shorter attention spans, and anxiety when we are away from screens. Our mission is to create tools that improve focus, nurture creativity, and support wellbeing. We design assistive technologies for children and adults, while promoting ethical standards and inclusive practices that bridge the gap in neurodiversity inclusion.
