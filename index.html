Who We Are

BIG FROOTS: conscious design for a more inclusive world

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to ethical technology — building tools that truly support mental health and neurodivergent inclusion rather than exploit human attention.

Many modern devices are engineered for maximum engagement, causing cognitive strain, shortened attention spans, and screen anxiety. We believe technology can do better.

Attention-Respecting Design

We build tools that improve focus, not undermine it. No addictive patterns, ever.

Inclusive by Default

Our products are designed first for children and adults with diverse cognitive needs.

Bridging the Gap

We promote ethical standards and inclusive practices across the EdTech landscape.

Nonprofit Mission

Every product we build serves our mission — not shareholder growth.
